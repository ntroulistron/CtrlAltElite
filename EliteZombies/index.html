About EliteZombies

In a world overrun by the undead, EliteZombies offers an unparalleled adventure of survival and strategy. Navigate through a post-apocalyptic city and use your wits and weapons to fend off the relentless zombie hordes. You will born in an area full of Zombies and you have to face and win every obstacle in order to gather points and escape!

Gameplay Mechanics

Control your survivor with intuitive mechanics, mastering combat through a combination of keyboard and mouse actions. From stealth to gunplay,from facing one zombies to facing multiple, your strategy will determine your fate.

Programming Langueges and Engines used.

This website and its accompanying game are crafted using a blend of modern web technologies and robust game development engines. The front-end of the website utilizes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure a responsive and dynamic user experience. For the game development aspect, we have employed Unity, a powerful game development platform known for its versatility and efficiency in building high-quality 2D and 3D games. Together, these technologies provide a seamless integration of web functionality and interactive gaming experiences.
